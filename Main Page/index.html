Dear students, thank you for choosing our college as your choice of Higher Education. As you might be aware that LOKD College is one of the oldest institutions in Sonitpur, Assam. Today the college has sprung up to be the best pioneering center for academic excellence. Affiliated to Gauhati University, the college provides not only a good education but also provides education, training and counselling in many extra-curricular activities that range from NCC, Indoor Sports and Open and Distant Learning

Lokanayak Omeo Kumar Das

Late Lokanayak Omeo Kumar Das, the pioneer force behind the establishment of this college was a great educationist who envisaged the need of Higher Education among the youth of the locality. His determination against all odds to bring the college into existence culminated in what we have today, a gem of an educational institution which have enlightened many a minds till date.
